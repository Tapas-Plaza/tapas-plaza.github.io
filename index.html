We are Jeannette and Johan. Nice to meet you! With our children Giovanni, Rosa and Jaume we left Holland to open our eatery Tapas Plaza in Nerja in 2006. Previously known as 'Sabor de Amor'.

Where Jeannette has a magic touch with people, Johan has with the Wok. As a heartwarming hostess and a qualified and passionate chef, we'd love to welcome you into what we call our Home!

Before we had children, we traveled a lot. And we lived in Thailand. The food we picked up from all these travels, we now bring to your table.
